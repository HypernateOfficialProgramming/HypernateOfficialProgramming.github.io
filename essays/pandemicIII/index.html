The Pandemic - A school essay (III)

During The 11th of March in the year 2020, The global COVID-19 outbreak was declared as a pandemic by World Health Organization. Everyone wAS wearing masks to shield themselves from the incoming invasion, a strain of viruses known as SARS-CoV-2.

People were dying left and right as many caught the infection, failling into terminal illness.

Almost all airports were closed due to this disaster, and the hoarding of resources became increasingly more common.

In some areas, hotels were converted into quarantine zones for the virus.

My area never realized this until February 2022, where many people were hospitalized.

The Grand Cure

Finally, a few months after the event, a cure was developed. Most if not all had two progressive shots, a primary and, after 6 months, a booster shot.

And just like that, everyone was immune now.
