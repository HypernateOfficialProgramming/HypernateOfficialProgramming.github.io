Overview

Astra Exploration is a game where you collect resources, build a rocket, and explore the universe!
The player is a Von Neumann probe that has landed on a habitable planet, and the goal is to get the resources to build a ship and explore!
The whole game takes place in a procedural universe with a few built-in systems.
